This project focuses on creating a dashboard that tracks diversity and inclusion metrics within a corporate environment. The dashboard helps in understanding the gender balance at the executive management level, measuring progress, and identifying areas for improvement in gender diversity.

Project Features

  • Tracking gender diversity across executive roles
